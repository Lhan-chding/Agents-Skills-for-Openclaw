# Reading Checklist

Use this checklist before delivering final interpretation.

- Paper section or formula has been explicitly identified.
- Any missing context has been declared instead of guessed.
- Every important symbol has a definition.
- Every derivation step is shown without hidden jumps.
- Discretization and boundary-condition assumptions are explicit when relevant.
- Loss/objective decomposition is explicit when relevant.
- Experiment setup, baselines, and metrics are correctly described when relevant.
- Content is tagged as `paper-meaning`, `assistant-explanation`, or `assistant-inference`.
- Remaining uncertainty is stated clearly.
